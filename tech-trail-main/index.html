About Tech In My Style

We are a team of passionate learners—a designer and two developers—on a mission to make technology education more accessible, practical, and exciting for everyone.

Driven by curiosity and creativity, we designed and built this platform ourselves, starting with wireframes in Figma and bringing it to life using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Every line of code and every design choice is crafted with one goal in mind:

To help others learn the way we wish we had.

We're not experts—just learners building for learners. And we're growing with every step, just like you.
